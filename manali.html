Manali Tour Itinerary

Manali, Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, Kullu, Naggar

❤

🚗 : Arrival in Manali – Local Leisure

  • Arrival: Check-in to your hotel
  • Visit: Hadimba Temple, Mall Road
  • Activities: Café hopping, street shopping
  • Dinner: At hotel or local restaurant
  • Overnight: Stay in Manali

🌄 : Solang Valley – Adventure Day

  • Visit: Solang Valley (13 km)
  • Activities: Paragliding, Ziplining, Ropeway
  • Optional: Atal Tunnel Drive
  • Overnight: Stay in Manali

❄️ : Rohtang Pass / Snow Point

  • Drive: Early start to Rohtang Pass (~3 hrs)
  • Enjoy: Snow, views, and activities
  • Stops: Gulaba, Marhi, Rahala Falls
  • Return: By evening to Manali
  • Overnight: Stay in Manali

🏕️ : Kullu – Rafting & Shawl Factory Visit

  • Drive: 1.5 hrs to Kullu
  • Activities: River Rafting, Shawl Factory Tour
  • Optional: Manikaran Sahib Visit
  • Return: By evening
  • Overnight: Stay in Manali

🖼️ : Naggar Castle & Local Culture

  • Drive: 22 km to Naggar
  • Visit: Naggar Castle, Roerich Art Gallery
  • Lunch: Scenic café
  • Evening: Leisure in Manali
  • Overnight: Stay in Manali

🚌: Departure from Manali

  • Check-out: After breakfast
  • Shopping: Souvenirs, apples, handicrafts
  • Transfer: Bus/train/cab for return

🎒 Travel Tips

  • Best Time: April–June (pleasant), Dec–Feb (snow)
  • Clothing: Carry warm layers always
  • Permits: Required for Rohtang Pass (book online)
  • Food: Try Siddu, Dham, Trout Fish, Momos
  • Pack: Sunscreen, ID, medicines, sunglasses
